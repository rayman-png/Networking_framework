◆ AEMtx33

typedef struct AEMtx33 AEMtx33

Matrix is stored in column major format, ie. the translation term is in the right most column.

m[0][0] m[0][1] m[0][2]
m[1][0] m[1][1] m[1][2]
m[2][0] m[2][1] m[2][2]
