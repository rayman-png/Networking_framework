Plays an audio.

Parameters
[in]audioThe audio to play.
[in]groupThe audio group to play the audio on.
[in]volumeThe volume of the audio to play. The range is from 0 to 1, where 0 represents 0% and 1 represents 100%
[in]pitchThe pitch of the audio to play. The range is from 0 to 1, where 0 represents 0% and 1 represents 100%
[in]loopsThe amount of time to loop the audio. 0 means that the audio will play once. 1 means that the audio will play twice. -1 means that the audio will loop forever.
